DROID VPN

STEP 1
  • Firstly..download droid vpn from the APPSTORE

STEP 2
  • After successful installation..., open app and tap "menu" then "account" and press "signup for free"
    After successful signup....An activation link will be sent to your email..open gmail app and activate your account
    When done...place your account and password in droid vpn

STEP 3
  • After saving account information...tap the "menu" icon again and click "update servers"
    NOTE:You must make sure you have an active internet connection

STEP 4
  • Tap "menu" icon...go to settings and tap "UDP CONNECTION SETTINGS" and under this...
    Remote port = 80,443 or 53
    Local port = 8080,1089 or 3128
    Disable port scan
    UDP connection mode = mode1
    T count = 4500
    R count = 5555

STEP 5
  • Press back then go to "VPN CONNECTION SETTINGS..
    Enable first three permissions
    MTU tunnel size = 1024

FINAL STEP
  • Return to home select a server of your own...if you have a free account..only connect using first 24 servers and if premium account...choose any server of your choice.
    Press "start" until it connects successfully
